COVER CROP CALCULATOR :-

This calculator can give you a precise idea about the mathematical aspects of the yields and other paramethers if you use one of the particular cover crops :-

Note: This Soil Health Calculator provides estimates based
on overall average values of measurements reported in more than 500 studies.
These studies represent a range of cropping systems and times since implementation
of winter cover crops. Therefore, actual results will likely vary.

PARAMETERS USED :-

Cash crop yield Cash crop yield for the season following winter cover crops. Positive (+) values mean that on average the cash crop will have a higher yield

Bulk density Mass of soil particles within a given volume. Negative (-) values are associated with more pore space and less compaction

Organic matter associated carbon in soil. Positive (+) values are associated with greater soil water holding capacity and better structure

Amount of nitrogen in the soil, including inorganic and organic forms Positive (+) values mean that less inorganic nitrogen may be required for the cash crop(s)

Ability of soil aggregates to hold together during disturbances such as rain. Positive (+) values indicate greater stability and more resistance to erosion and crusting

Rate at which water can enter into the soil. Positive (+) values indicate that soil will absorb more water and have less overland flow during rain

Saturated hydraulic conductivity, which controls the rate of water movement through soil. Positive (+) values indicate that water will move with less resistance into and through soil

Movement and/or loss of soil due to wind or rain. Negative (-) values mean that less soil is affected by erosion

Water that ponds up on the soil surface and moves as overland flow. Negative (-) values means less overland flow, meaning less erosion and greater replenishment of soil water

Water that drains below the root zone. Negative (-) values mean less leaching, which can reduce loss of fertilizers and herbicides/pesticides from soil

Biomass associated with weeds during the following cash crop growing season. Negative (-) values mean that fewer weeds were found in the field after cover crops

Carbon associated with soil microbes. Positive (+) values are associated with beneficial biological functions and more plant available carbon

Nitrogen associated with soil microbes. Positive (+) values are associated with beneficial biological functions and more plant available nitrogen
